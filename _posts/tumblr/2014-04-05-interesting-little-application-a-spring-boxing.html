Interesting little application: a spring boxing glove oscillates back and forth on the level indicated by the mouse/touch position. It has literally no purpose, and “Hello World” is also displayed in tiny red letters.

The idea came from my platformer fighting game idea which uses the same kind of weapon. I was trying to wrap my head around rendering the spring portion of the weapon: which is slightly tricky, since I had to simulate rotation and translation with line coordinates to maintain uniform lengths. Combined with the oscillations, I had to use a bit of physics and geometry, which made me feel pretty cool.

I did it in a little under an hour. Nothing much was learned other than the ShapeRenderer class, which I used to render the lines. But created small applications as such is great practice anyway.

The sprite is from Spinmaster, and was taken from The Spriter’s Resource.
