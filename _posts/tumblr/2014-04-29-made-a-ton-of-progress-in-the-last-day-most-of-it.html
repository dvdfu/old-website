Made a ton of progress in the last day. Most of it is documented on Github but the gist is I’ve fully nailed down movement (still “corner” cases that need some addressing, literally).

There’s a lot more consideration involved for physics objects such as jumping springs, or moving platforms, but I’ll get to those when I have to. For now, 4-sided collision and jump-through-the-bottom-platforms have been created. The player movement is accelerated and the camera pans smoothly. Everything is going well so far.

I do wish I knew how efficient my methods are, though. I’ve tried to optimize calculations but I don’t know how well they will fare when the game is scaled up. Tile maps begin tearing when a full background of tiles is rendered. I’ll just have to see what can be done.
