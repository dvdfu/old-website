Enemy designs! I’ll call them fire goblins for now.Â The enemies needed to quickly convey three things:

  1. They could be attacked by jumping on them
  2. When jumped on, their stunned bodies could be used as a projectile, very much like Koopas and Koopa shells. In this case, the fire goblins retreat inside their rock and pretty much look like just a rock
  3. They need to be hot. I needed a reason to show that if they went into the lava in an un-stunned state, they would raise the level of lava. It still doesn’t make perfect sense, but it will suffice

There are also other things to consider: how hard will they be to animate? How can a player distinguish between different types of enemies? I’ve shown two examples here of a basic walking fire goblin, and a legless one that jumps high and moves like a frog.

Neither of the designs are final, but they’re the closest I’ve got to finalized so far.
