Alright! This game is going places. I swapped in some Mario Bros. sprites because I don’t have any of my own, yet. I realized I really dislike creating animations for games…mainly because I’m no good at it. Anyway, here’s some details and commentary.

  1. I tweaked the gameplay a lot from the previous screenshot set. Stunned enemies escape stun after 3 seconds and in general are used a lot differently. But the principle is more or less the same: stomp on enemies to stun, grabbed stunned enemies and throw them to kill.
  2. Added the rising floor. It takes 5 enemies entering the lava for it to completely engulf the first floor. If the player is quick, they can reach the second floor and have another 3 enemies before guaranteed death, but at this point, it’s significantly harder to keep enemies out.

Here are some things that I tried that did NOT work:

  • Wrapping levels: players and enemies walk through the left side, come out the right, and vice-versa. Failed because it made the game too easy. The player’s position and throwing angle hardly mattered, and there was no real decision making
  • Enemy HP: some enemies took multiple hits to kill, but this was far too difficult to keep track of. A player should see an enemy and always know it’s just one hit away from death
  • "Flowerpot": I toyed with an idea where instead of preventing enemies from falling off the stage, you have to prevent them from touching a flowerpot (which you could also carry around like an enemy). Way too difficult, and it encouraged staying in one spot all the time
