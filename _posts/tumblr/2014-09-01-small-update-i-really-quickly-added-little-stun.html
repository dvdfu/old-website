Small update. I really quickly added little stun stars above enemies when they’re stunned. The number of stars indicates how many seconds will pass before they resume activity. I enjoy including small details like these!

The enemy sprites changed a bit since last time. I realized adding lighting to the left or right of the sprite made it look very bad in-game, implying a source of light that’s inconsistent to everything else. Plus, the sprites were a bit too noisy, so I reduced the total number of colours used (even more).

On a side note, when the enemies are stunned, they are awfully bouncy…
